Privacy Policy

Last updated 12 May 2026

This is a short summary. The full policy below covers everything the GDPR requires.

The short version

  • We only collect the data we need to run Paul & Me.
  • We never sell your data, full stop.
  • Servers and backups live in the EU (Germany).
  • You can export everything or delete your account at any time.

1. Controller

Paul an Me GmbH, Borsigstraße 26, 73249 Wernau, Germany. Contact for privacy questions: privacy@pauland.me.

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: name, email, password (hashed), company info.
  • Property data: what you tell Paul about your listing — address, photos, rates, calendar.
  • Booking data: guest details collected via your widget (necessary to fulfil the booking).
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used. Aggregated, no third-party analytics.
  • Billing data: processed by Stripe; we store only the last 4 digits + invoice metadata.

3. Legal basis

Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract) for everything required to operate your account, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest) for fraud prevention and product analytics, and Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR (consent) for non-essential cookies and marketing.

4. How long we keep it

Account data: until you delete your account, plus 30 days of grace. Booking and invoice data: 10 years (HGB §257, AO §147). Logs: 14 days, then aggregated.

5. Your rights

Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, withdrawal of consent. Email privacy@pauland.me and we'll handle it within 30 days. You can also complain to your local supervisory authority (e.g. LfDI Baden-Württemberg).

Terms of Service

AGB · Last updated 12 May 2026

1. About these terms

These are the General Terms (AGB) between you and Paul an Me GmbH ("Paul & Me", "we"). They apply when you sign up for an account on pauland.me.

2. The service

Paul & Me is a subscription tool for managing vacation rental bookings: a booking widget, a calendar, a guest inbox and a pricing module. We don't take commission on bookings — only the monthly subscription fee.

3. Account & trial

A free 14-day trial of the Expert plan is available to new accounts. No card needed. If you don't subscribe, the account pauses at day 15 and data is retained for 30 days.

4. Subscription & payment

Monthly plans renew each month; annual plans renew each year. Prices are listed exclusive of VAT on pricing.html. Payments are processed by Stripe.

5. Cancellation

You can cancel any time from Settings → Plan. Monthly plans stop at the end of the current billing month; annual plans stop at the end of the billing year. We won't refund mid-cycle, but we won't charge again.

6. Acceptable use

Don't use Paul & Me to list properties you don't have the right to rent out, to facilitate illegal activity, or to spam your guests. We reserve the right to suspend accounts that violate these rules.

7. Liability

Paul & Me is provided "as is" with reasonable care. We're liable for damages caused by gross negligence or intent, and within the limits required by German law. We are not liable for lost bookings due to brief service outages.

8. Governing law

German law applies. Place of jurisdiction is Stuttgart, Germany, for merchants. Consumers retain the right to bring claims at their local court.

Cookies

Last updated 12 May 2026

Paul & Me uses a small number of cookies. None of them follow you around the internet.

Strictly necessary

  • pam_session — keeps you logged in. Expires when you log out.
  • pam_csrf — protects against cross-site request forgery. Expires on browser close.

Functional (with your consent)

  • pam_prefs — remembers your dashboard preferences (theme, language). 12 months.

No third-party tracking

We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any cross-site tracker. Our internal usage stats are aggregated server-side and never tied to your identity.

You can withdraw your consent at any time from Settings → Privacy.
