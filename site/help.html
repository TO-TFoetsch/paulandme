Getting started The basics — from sign-up to your first booking.

How long does setup take?

Most hosts have their first listing live within an afternoon. The longest part is uploading photos — Paul handles the rest with smart defaults you can tune later.

Do I need my own website?

No. If you don't have a site, Paul gives you a hosted booking link you can put in your Instagram bio, email signature or print materials.

How do I embed the booking widget?

From your dashboard → Widget → Embed. Copy one line of code into your website. WordPress, Squarespace, plain HTML — all supported.

Bookings & calendar Sync, blocking, and avoiding double bookings.

Does Paul sync with Airbnb / Booking.com?

Yes, via iCal. Add your Airbnb iCal URL in Calendar → Sync. Paul reads incoming bookings and exposes its own iCal feed for the other direction.

How does Paul prevent double bookings?

The widget shows live availability. The moment a booking lands, Paul updates your calendar and the external iCal feed within 30 seconds.

Can I block individual dates?

Yes. Click and drag in the calendar to block, hold or open a range. Add a note (e.g. "owner's stay") so you remember why.

Pricing & payments Rates, taxes, payouts.

Is there really no commission?

Yes, really. You pay a monthly subscription — nothing per booking. Whatever your guest pays lands in your account minus payment-processor fees, which Paul doesn't take a cut of either.

How do I get paid?

Connect Stripe (cards) or SEPA (bank transfer) in Settings → Payments. Payouts go directly to your bank account on a schedule you choose.

Does Paul handle VAT?

Yes. Set your VAT rate per property. Auto-generated invoices include the correct breakdown for German and EU bookings.

Account & billing Plan, password, cancellation.

How do I switch plans?

Settings → Plan. Switch up or down at any time — the change applies at your next billing cycle.

How do I cancel my account?

Settings → Plan → Cancel. No phone calls, no retention agents. Your data stays for 30 days in case you change your mind, then we delete it.

I forgot my password — what now?

From the login page click "Forgot password?". We'll email you a reset link valid for 60 minutes.

Can't find what you're looking for?

Paul's team is small but quick. Most tickets get a reply within 2 hours during business days.
