FAQ

Common questions, short answers

Is there really no commission?

Yes, really. You pay the monthly subscription, that's it. Whatever your guest pays for the booking, lands in your account (minus the payment processor's fee — which we don't take a cut of either).

Can I cancel at any time?

Anytime. Monthly plans stop at the end of the current billing month, annual plans run until the end of the billing year. No phone calls, no retention agents.

What happens during my 14-day trial?

You get the full Expert plan for 14 days. No card on file. If you do nothing, the trial ends and your account quietly pauses — your data stays for 30 days in case you change your mind.

Can I switch between Foundation and Expert?

Of course. Switch up or down at any time from your dashboard — the change applies on your next billing cycle.

Does Paul work with my existing website?

Yep. Drop a single <script> tag into your site (we handle the rest), or share a hosted link if you don't have a site yet.
