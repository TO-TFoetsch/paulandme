Site settings

These options control how the whole website is presented. They are saved in this browser and applied on every page.

Theme

Cream is the standard brand look; Navy is the dark variant.

Hand illustrations

The signature Paul hands on heroes and CTA bands.

Text contrast

Accessible darkens text-sized pink and captions to meet WCAG AA (4.5:1).

Animations

Scroll reveals and card hover lifts. Users with a reduced-motion preference never see them, regardless of this setting.

Hero variant

Homepage only — A: pink circle & thumbs-up, B: stat card & quote, C: phone mockup.

Defaults: Cream · hands on · accessible contrast · animations on · hero A
